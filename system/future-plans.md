# Future Plans & Growth Opportunities

*Things deliberately excluded from the current build — not because they're bad ideas, but because building them would consume the six months meant for learning. Revisit after Post #12.*

---

## Platform & Infrastructure

**PDF / Academic Paper System**
Local PDF storage organized by month, with metadata, read status, and annotation support. Potential Zotero-compatible structure. Could integrate arXiv/PubMed/DOI fetchers for paper discovery. Significant build — not worth the time tradeoff now.

**Time Tracking**
Hours logged per reading session, weekly totals, monthly totals, streak tracking. Useful for calibrating the 5–10 hrs/week estimate against reality. Simple to build with a markdown log; complex to build with a real UI.

**Full Knowledge System**
Backlinks, concept tags, cross-resource linking — something closer to Obsidian or a personal research wiki. The current markdown structure handles 12 posts cleanly. If the project continues past Month 6, this becomes worth building.

**Local AI Integration**
Local model or API for paper summarization, jargon definition, "explain this like I'm a PM" prompts. Currently handled in-session via Claude. If CO extends to a second phase or becomes a teaching resource, a built-in AI layer would add real value.

## Content Features

**Audio Production**
Scripts adapted from posts, recorded and hosted. Architecture is already in `system/agents/audio-producer.md`. Activation checklist is defined — just needs a mic setup decision and 30 minutes of test production to validate the workflow.

**Second Phase: Deeper Specialization**
If Month 6 produces a clear preferred subfield (developmental psych, social psych, evolutionary psych), a Phase 2 curriculum built around that subfield is a natural next step. Could include primary paper-reading cadence (no book spine), a research proposal draft, and direct engagement with faculty at target programs.

**Multi-Topic Course Structure**
The current build is one 6-month course. The architecture could support multiple sequential courses (Behavioral Science 101 → Research Methods → Specialized Subfield). Not needed for the litmus test, but the folder structure already supports it.

## Community & Accountability (Someday)

A private cohort of 3–5 people running the same curriculum simultaneously — shared reflection prompts, monthly discussions, accountability without public performance pressure. Consistent with the zero-monetization framing of the current project. Only worth building if Month 6 produces a clear "continue" answer.
